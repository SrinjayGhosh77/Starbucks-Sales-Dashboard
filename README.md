# Starbucks-Sales-Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed using a Starbucks sales dataset. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, product categories, store performance, customer purchasing behavior, and overall business trends. It helps identify key growth opportunities through interactive visualizations, KPI cards, and business-focused analysis.

## Business Questions Answered

### Sales Performance

* What are the total sales, orders, and quantity sold?
* How do sales change over time?
* Which month generates the highest sales?
* Which day of the week has the highest sales?
* Which hour of the day records the highest customer traffic?

### Product Analysis

* Which product category generates the highest sales?
* Which products are the top-selling items?
* Which products contribute the highest revenue?
* Which product categories perform below expectations?

### Store Analysis

* Which store location generates the highest sales?
* Which store has the highest number of orders?
* How does store performance compare across different locations?

### Customer Purchase Analysis

* What are the peak purchasing hours?
* What are the busiest days for Starbucks stores?
* Which products are most frequently purchased together (if applicable)?

### Business Insights

* Which products drive the majority of revenue?
* Which stores contribute the most to overall business performance?
* What sales trends can help improve business decisions?
